Who We Are?


MagicTicket is your all-in-one digital platform for discovering and booking circus shows. We connect circus lovers with unforgettable performances, making ticket reservations quick, easy, and accessible for everyone.

Born from the passion of students at the University of Costa Rica, our platform blends creativity, innovation, and a love for the performing arts to bring you the best circus experiences.

Our Mission

To make the magic of the circus accessible to everyone, everywhere. We aim to simplify the ticket booking process while supporting the circus community and spreading joy through entertainment.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital platform for circus shows worldwide—empowering artists, delighting audiences, and keeping the circus spirit alive in the digital age.

Our Core Values

Accessibility

We believe everyone should enjoy the circus, no matter where they are. That’s why we’ve built an easy-to-use, fast, and responsive platform for all types of users.

Passion for Entertainment

We live for magic, laughter, and the thrill only a circus can bring. Our team is dedicated to creating unforgettable user experiences—every step of the way.

Trust & Security

We take your privacy and security seriously. MagicTicket offers a secure, reliable space for managing your data and transactions with complete confidence.


Our Team

We are a group of enthusiastic Computer Engineering students from the University of Costa Rica, united by our shared love for innovation and showbiz. With backgrounds in software development, user experience design, and event coordination, we’ve come together to create a platform that redefines how circus fans connect with their favorite shows.

Our diversity fuels our creativity, and our technical skills drive the performance behind MagicTicket.
